About CC

Campus Connection has a big data trend analysis powered marketplace for college students. Limiting the buyers and sellers to within their university creates a more secure buying and selling environment by ensuring a common background among the participants. Each item being bought or sold will come with university specific metrics of the item such as average price and amount sold the previous week, enabling buyers to choose fair prices and sellers to feel confident. CC will also provide a location sharing service, so students can quickly send or receive the locations of other students on campus.

Price Point Visibility

Product Price Visibility (PPV) feature will guarantee sellers to know the best price to sell their item, and for buyers the security of knowing they are paying the correct amount. Using text searching techniques that will match similar string patterns together, CC will use the information from new daily ads to show users at what price similar products are being sold at.
